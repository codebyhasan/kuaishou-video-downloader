# Kuaishou Video Downloader

## Summary
Extract and download videos from Kuaishou (快手)

## Description
This extension allows you to easily extract and download videos from Kuaishou (快手) for offline viewing. 

### Features

**Quick Access:**
- Click the extension icon in your browser toolbar to instantly open Kuaishou's recommendation feed (new-reco page)
- No need to manually navigate to the website

**Video Detection:**
- Automatically detects videos on Kuaishou feed pages
- Works on individual short video pages (/short-video/)
- Shows real-time detection status in the widget

**Download Options:**
- Download video files directly to your device
- Download thumbnail images separately
- Download both video and thumbnail with one click
- Uses efficient blob downloading with progress indicator

**Information Display:**
- View video title, creator name, and video ID
- See direct video URL and thumbnail URL
- Get the Kuaishou short link for sharing
- Preview the output filename before downloading

**Clipboard Integration:**
- Click any field (title, creator, URL, etc.) to copy it to your clipboard
- Easily share video information with friends

**Smart Design:**
- Clean, modern dark UI that matches Kuaishou's aesthetic
- Floating widget that doesn't obstruct your viewing experience
- Smooth animations and transitions
- Works seamlessly with Kuaishou's responsive layout

### How to Use
1. Click the extension icon to open Kuaishou
2. Scroll to any video in your feed or open a video page
3. Click the download button to save the video
4. Or click any field to copy its content

### Permissions Required
- **Clipboard:** Used to copy video information to your clipboard
- **Tabs:** Used to open Kuaishou when clicking the extension icon

### Privacy
This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any user data. All video downloads are performed directly in your browser. No analytics, no tracking, no external servers.

## Privacy Policy
This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any user data. All video downloads are performed directly in the browser.

## Notes to Reviewer
This extension downloads videos from Kuaishou for offline viewing. It uses the clipboard API to copy video information and tabs API to open Kuaishou pages. No user data is collected or transmitted to any external servers.
